Help & Support

Getting Started

Welcome to TaskMitra! Here are a few quick tips to get you started:

  • Click the "Add Task" button on any page to create a new task.
  • Navigate between pages using the sidebar on the left.
  • Organize your tasks using the Task Categories page.
  • Customize your experience in the Settings page.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I edit an existing task?

To edit a task, go to the "My Tasks" page, click on the task card to open the detailed view, and then click the "Edit Task" button. This will open a modal where you can modify the task's details.

Can I use this app offline?

Yes! TaskMitra is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with offline capabilities. After your first visit, the app will be cached, allowing you to view and create tasks even without an internet connection. Any changes you make offline will sync automatically when you reconnect.

How do I change the theme?

You can change the theme by navigating to the "Settings" page. Under the "Appearance" section, you can choose between Dark, Light, or System default themes.

What are Vital Tasks?

The "Vital Task" page is designed to highlight your most critical, high-priority tasks. This helps you focus on what's most important without the distraction of other lower-priority items.
